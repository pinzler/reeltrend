Nothing to Install or Worry About
ReelTrend is online software, which means you can use it as long as you have an Internet connection and a modern web browser. There is nothing to install for you or your organization. The ReelTrend team monitors the application 24/7 to ensure its availability, reliability and security.
Comprehensive current information
ReelTrend partners with leading film review, distribution, and social media sites to provide you with the most current snapshot of how people are feeling about your film. We are always adding more data sources which means that the information is reliable and accurate.
